As a Teaching Assistant at LUMS, I supported undergraduate students in mathematics courses through weekly tutorials, office hours, grading, and individualised academic guidance. This role deepened my understanding of pedagogical challenges at university level and sharpened my ability to explain complex ideas in accessible ways.
