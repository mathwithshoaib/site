As an Instructor

Teaching mathematics at university level — making rigorous ideas accessible, intuitive, and inspiring.

Fall 2025 · LUMS

Math-101 Calculus-I

Adjunct Teaching Fellow alongside Dr. Imran Anwar

I started my first course as an instructor at LUMS — teaching Calculus-I (Math-101) alongside the lead instructor Dr. Imran Anwar. The course covered limits, derivatives, integration, and differential equations, taught to a class of undergraduate students from diverse academic backgrounds.

I designed interactive lecture notes, held weekly office hours, prepared problem sets, and graded examinations. Calculus-1 Fall 2025 final marks have been uploaded.
