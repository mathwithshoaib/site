Track A

Rigorous Track

Epsilon-delta definitions, formal proofs, classical analysis. Based on Stewart's Calculus.

Track B

Applied Track

Business, economics & social science applications. Based on Hoffmann's Calculus.

Topics Covered

Limits & Continuity

What it means for a function to approach a value. Formal definitions, limit laws, continuity.

Differentiation

Rates of change, tangent lines, all differentiation rules, and applications to optimisation.

Integration

Antiderivatives, Riemann sums, the Fundamental Theorem, and techniques of integration.

Differential Equations

Separable DEs, initial value problems, and continuous compounding.
