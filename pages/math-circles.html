Math Circles

Bringing the joy and wonder of mathematics to young minds — problem-solving, mathematical puzzles, and the art of thinking mathematically.

🔵

Math Circles — Facilitator

Math Circles is an enrichment programme that gathers students outside the standard curriculum to explore mathematics through puzzles, problems, and guided discovery. As a facilitator, I designed and led interactive sessions on topics ranging from number theory and combinatorics to geometry and mathematical reasoning.

The philosophy of Math Circles is that mathematics is best learned when it feels like play — when the student is genuinely curious and the problem genuinely surprising. Sessions were designed to produce exactly that feeling.

Mathematical PuzzlesNumber TheoryCombinatoricsGeometryYouth Outreach
Why Math Circles matters: Most students never get to experience mathematics as mathematicians actually do it — as an adventure of discovery, surprise, and beauty. Math Circles gives them that experience.
