Hobbies

Life outside equations — the things that restore, inspire, and remind me why mathematics is beautiful.

Mathematics history, popular science, and literature. Currently reading about the lives of great mathematicians and the stories behind major theorems.

✈️

Travel

Lived in France, studied in Pakistan's finest cities. Travel broadens both the mind and the mathematical intuition — every culture has its own way of thinking.

🎯

Problem Solving

Mathematical olympiad problems, puzzles, and recreational mathematics. There is no better hobby for a mathematician than mathematics itself.

🏋️

Fitness

Staying active keeps the mind sharp. A healthy body supports a healthy mathematical mind.

🎨

Visual Design

Creating educational materials, slides, and infographics with Canva. Mathematics deserves beautiful presentation.

💻

Coding

Python, MATLAB, and Mathematica for mathematical exploration. Building interactive lecture notes with HTML and JavaScript.
