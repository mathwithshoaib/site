Education

A path through Pakistan's finest institutions, France, and Italy — shaped by rigorous mathematics and international exposure.

2021–2022

M1 Mathématiques

University of Lille · Lille, France

I had the privilege of spending a transformative year in France as a recipient of a fully funded scholarship for the M1 program at the University of Lille. This highly competitive fellowship saw only two students selected from across Pakistan, and I am honoured to be one of them. This immersive year broadened my academic horizons, immersed me in advanced mathematical analysis, and exposed me to a vibrant cross-cultural academic environment.

Fully Funded Scholarship 1 of 2 from Pakistan Mathématiques France
🌍
2019–2021

MS Mathematics — IMM Program

COMSATS Lahore · in collaboration with ICTP Italy

I completed my master's under the prestigious International Mathematics Master (IMM) program — the first of its kind in Pakistan — launched in 2019 through a collaboration between COMSATS Lahore and ICTP Italy. Only 10 students were selected from across Pakistan, along with 2 international students, for the inaugural batch. I was honoured to be among them, taught by an international faculty from across Europe.

Thesis: The Fundamental Group and Classification of Covering Spaces

IMM Program ICTP Italy Collaboration Algebraic Topology First Batch

MSc Mathematics — Gold Medal

Government College University (GCU) · Lahore, Pakistan

I graduated with the Gold Medal in my MSc Mathematics from GCU Lahore — awarded for securing the highest academic standing in my cohort. This honour reflects consistent dedication, strong analytical ability, and a deep passion for mathematics. It has been a defining milestone in my academic journey, motivating me to pursue advanced studies internationally.
