Current Focus

Research in Mathematical Epidemiology

My doctoral research develops compartmental ODE models for disease co-infection — studying how pathogens like influenza and pneumonia interact in shared host populations. The work involves computing $\mathcal{R}_0$ via the Next Generation Matrix, stability analysis, and bifurcation theory.
